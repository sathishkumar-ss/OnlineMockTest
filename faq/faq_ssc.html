Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

Q.1) When is the notice/advertisement of an Examination issued?

Tentative dates of issue of Notices of different Examinations are given in the Annual Calendar of Examinations. It is therefore advised to regularly visit the website of the Commission for latest updates.

Q.2) How many times I can modify my SSC registration?

Login using your 'Registration Number' and 'SSC Registration Password' in the login section of the website. Click on 'Modify Registration' and make necessary changes. Be cautious while editing as entries in the 'Basic Details' tab are allowed modification only once.

Q.3) Is it possible to register SSC again if we have already registered?

If you have already registered for SSC, you cannot register again. However, you can modify or change the details entered during the registration process. While filling the application form, the details entered during registration are visible and you can edit them if required.

Q.4) Can I register twice for SSC?

Next time when you need to apply for any exam conducted by ssc you don't have to register again. Advantage of this database maintenance is that it saves precious time of candidates because they don't need to fill the same details again and again. No you should not register twice

Q.5) What is provisionally accepted in SSC?

The words “Provisionally Accepted” at the top of already printed applications should be clearly seen as an acknowledgement and not an acceptance. The candidates are advised to re-read the instructions and re-take the printouts.

Q.6) Can I change my category from EWS to general in SSC?

No, the EWS candidate, other than those recommended on General Merit, shall not be allowed to change their category from EWS to Unreserved/General or claim the vacancies (Service/Cadre) for Unreserved/General category after the declaration of final result by the UPSC.

Q.7) Can I get rejected during document verification?

Reason for rejection during document verification is as follows: Name of candidate in the application/E-admit card is not as per Class 10thcertificate, 12th Certificate, Diploma certificate, Identity card, SC/ST/OBC/EWS Certificate and any other certificate as submitted/uploaded in the online application.
