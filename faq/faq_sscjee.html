Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

Q1: I registered for JEE (Advanced) 2021 but did not appear for both paper 1 and paper 2. Do I have to qualify JEE (Main) 2022 in order to appear for JEE (Advanced) 2022?

You are directly eligible for JEE (Advanced) 2022 under special one-time measure. However, you are required to register for JEE (Advanced) 2022 in the online registration portal. (See 11. Eligibility Criteria for Indian Nationals for Appearing in JEE (Advanced) 2022 in Information Brochure)

Q2: I registered for JEE (Advanced) 2021 but was absent in only one of the papers. Do I have to qualify JEE (Main) 2022 in order to appear for JEE (Advanced) 2022?

Appearing in one of the papers is also considered as an attempt. You must qualify JEE (Main) 2022 in order to be eligible for JEE (Advanced) 2022. You also need to satisfy all the other eligibility criteria (See 11. Eligibility Criteria for Indian Nationals for Appearing in JEE (Advanced) 2022 in Information Brochure).

Q3: I appeared for Class XII examination for the first time in 2021. Am I eligible for JEE (Advanced) 2022?

You are eligible for appearing in JEE (Advanced) 2022 provided you also satisfy all the eligibility criteria mentioned in the Information Brochure (see 11. Eligibility Criteria for Indian Nationals for Appearing in JEE (Advanced) 2022).

Q5: I appeared for Class XII examination for the first time in 2020 and my Class XII examination board declared the results before October 15, 2020. I appeared for neither JEE (Advanced) 2020 nor JEE (Advanced) 2021. Am I eligible for JEE (Advanced) 2022?

You are eligible for JEE (Advanced) 2022 as a one-time measure approved by the JAB provided you also satisfy all the eligibility criteria mentioned in the Information Brochure (See 11. Eligibility Criteria for Indian Nationals for Appearing in JEE (Advanced) 2022).

I appeared for Class XII examination for the first time in 2020 and my Class XII examination board declared the results before October 15, 2020. I did not appear for JEE (Advanced) 2020 but appeared for JEE(Advanced) for the first time in 2021. Am I eligible for JEE (Advanced) 2022?

You are eligible for JEE (Advanced) 2022 as a one-time measure approved by the JAB provided you also satisfy all the eligibility criteria mentioned in the Information Brochure (See 11. Eligibility Criteria for Indian Nationals for Appearing in JEE (Advanced) 2022).
