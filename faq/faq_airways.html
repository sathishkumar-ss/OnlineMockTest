Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

Q.1) Can a male also become cabin crew?

Yes a male can also become a cabin crew.

Q.2) What are the requirements of becoming a cabing crew or air hostess?

Minimum 18 years of age and 12th pass in any stream.

Q.3) Are there any scholarships available to become a pilot?

Yes there are different scholarships which are provided by state and central governments. one can check with department of social jusctice and empowerment. Note: The scholarship amount is not sufficient to complete your pilot training.

Q.4) How much money is required to become a pilot?

There is no such fixed amount but approx 40-50l according to current scenarios.

Q.5) What if i am not able to clear the DGCA exams in first attempt?

No worries, you can again appear for the exams. There is no such limit on giving DGCA attempts.

Q.6) What kinds of trainings are imparted by AAI?

Indian Aviation Academy Indian Aviation Academy also known by its acronym as IAA, is a premier training institute and a joint Training Academy of Airports Authority of India, BCAS & DGCA, established in the year 2009. It imparts training in all disciplines of airport management i.e. airport operations, airport engineering construction, maintenance / project management, airport finance, human resource management, airport commercial /Land Management, Aviation law, and Cargo Management, Aviation Safety & Security. It is an IATA accredited Training School for imparting DGR training course and IATA authorized Training Centre for Basic Cargo Introductory course.
